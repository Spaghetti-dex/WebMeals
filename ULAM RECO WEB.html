Explore delicious Filipino recipes and discover your next favorite dish!

Adobo

A classic Filipino dish made with chicken or pork in a savory soy sauce and vinegar sauce.

Sinigang

A sour soup with tamarind as the base, often with pork or shrimp.

Kare-Kare

A thick, savory peanut stew often paired with bagoong (shrimp paste).
